Data Tables: Grow as they Grow approach

Introduction

This case study focuses on enhancing the user experience of Chargebee's Index Page, with a specific emphasis on accommodating the diverse needs of both startup and enterprise customers. The aim is to streamline the data table interface while expanding its functionality as businesses grow.

This case study is my own and does not necessarily reflect the views of Chargebee. This project was self-initiated and not a part any initiative in Chargebee nor was it published anywhere.

Personas and User needs

There are primarily three main uses for Index Page - Creation, Maintenance and Overview.

New Startup Business (65% of customer base)

  • Primary focus on creating customers.
  • Need to understand Chargebee and build confidence.

Growth Stage Users

  • Expansion of internal teams and focus on function specific job tasks. Example: Finance persona for managing invoices. Support executive handling customer requests.

C-level Executives

  • Require an overview of customers and business performance.
  • Focus on internal team insights.

The Problem

The challenge is to redesign the experience to cater to startups while expanding features as their business grows.

For the purpose of simplyfing and narrowing down the scope of this exercise, I have taken only the Subscription Index Page.

Design Principles

Efficiency
Data tables must be well-organised to ensure ease of content navigation and offer various interactive intuitive solutions to find information easily
Scalability
Data tables must be flexible to accommodate large volumes of data while maintaining their usability
Feedback
Data tables must reflect with information about the actions users have taken and the results of those actions to make them feel in control
Design Output
Wireframes

Empty state

  • Using empty state to educate the users what they can expect here

1 page data

  • When there are only a few data, creation can still be the primary button
  • Simplify the functionality to help onboarding -Saving filtered view seem unnecessary at this point

More than 1 page of data

  • Pagination to help users navigate and find data
  • Create milestone to give sense of progress and introduce features that will be more suitable for that stage of business and user. With Views feature, user can now save filters for easy access
  • Select All feature, with pagination it can get difficult to select all at once. A small enhancement to improve efficiency.
  • Contextual actions appear when a selection has occured

When custom fields have been created

  • Flexibility to show and hide columns and rearrange them
  • Persistent Row level actions to enable quick action on the record
  • Multiple interactions to re-arrange columns
  • Persistent horizontal scrolling bar on the table for easy access on all systems

When many views are created

  • Introduce Dashboard view with tables and data based on views created and user persona
Conclusion:

This redesign prioritises user efficiency, scalability, and feedback. It acknowledges that each customer's journey is unique and ensures they can make the most of Chargebee's features, regardless of their business stage.
